Transparency

We set out to solve common problems faced by crypto users. Our solutions are simple and straightforward, and they go beyond trading to also support companies and institutions in the cryptosphere.

Business

We are offering the technology we developed to other crypto projects and businesses, aiming to provide a complete solution for everything from crowd sale to token utility through staking & governance, and beyond.

Wallet

Never lose access to your wallet with the ability to link two wallets together.
Give access for a master wallet to have full control over other wallets after a set time period.

OTC

On-Chain Escrow also offers the possibility of easily requesting funds. When using this feature in a send-request manner, it gives the possibility of trustless, 100% DeFi over-the-counter (OTC) trading.

Governance

Anyone holding 1 EBOX or above at a certain point in time is eligible to vote. Each vote, takes a snapshot of the blockchain from one to few days before the voting and will be used to determine the list of eligible voters.
